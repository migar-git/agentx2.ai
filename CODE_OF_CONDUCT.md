# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are building an AI-native company in the open. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free,
respectful, and inclusive experience for everyone — contributors and AI agents' human operators
alike.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and respect.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community.
- Grounding disagreements in facts and evidence.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or work.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is
representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **<conduct@agentx2.ai>**. Maintainers will review and respond
promptly, and will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
