## 1. Purpose

Defines the business model, service catalog, revenue streams, and operating model. Source-grounded in
[`PRD_AgentX2.md`](../../PRD_AgentX2.md) and [`README.md`](../../README.md).

## 2. Business model

Subscription-first AI transformation partner: continuous value (retainers, managed agents, AI
operations) rather than one-time engagements, augmented by project-based build work.

## 4. Revenue streams

1. **Subscriptions** — Starter, Growth, Enterprise tiers.
2. **Managed AI Workforce™** — digital workers as a service.
3. **Project build** — fixed-scope agent/automation delivery.
4. **Advisory retainers** — executive AI leadership.
5. **Future product** — platform + agent marketplace (see [Roadmap](../09-roadmap/ROADMAP.md)).

## 5. Subscription tiers

| Tier | For | Includes |
|------|-----|----------|
| Starter | SMB beginning AI | readiness assessment, monthly advisory, opportunity ID |
| Growth | Implementing AI | dedicated consultant, solution architecture, deployment support, KPI reporting |
| Enterprise | Scaling AI | dedicated team, multi-agent solutions, integrations, governance, continuous optimization |
| Managed AI Workforce™ | Digital workforce | agent fleet, monitoring, optimization, governance, reporting |

## 6. Operating model (agent-enabled)

Every internal function maps to an agent or swarm (executive, revenue, marketing, delivery,
engineering, operations, governance). The company eats its own dog food: it is built and run by the
same swarms it sells. See [Agentic Swarm](../01-architecture/AGENTIC_SWARM.md).

## 7. Industries served

Financial services · healthcare · manufacturing · technology · professional services
(per [`README.md`](../../README.md)).

## 8. Why us

AI-native · outcome-focused · subscription model · enterprise-ready (security, compliance,
governance) · vendor-agnostic · end-to-end delivery.
