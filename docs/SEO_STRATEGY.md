## 2. Summary

- How AgentX2.ai earns organic discovery: topic clusters, authority pages, and a programmatic SEO factory.
- Grounded in Google Search Essentials and structured with schema.org.
- Topic clusters (pillar → spokes): Agentic AI, AI for Finance, AI Governance, AI Automation, and Industries — each pillar is an authority page with interlinked spokes (≤3 clicks).
- On-page standard: unique title + meta, canonical URLs, schema.org types (`Organization`, `Service`, `FAQPage`, `BreadcrumbList`, `Article`), semantic headings, and descriptive alt text.
- `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt` are auto-generated; performance is an SEO input, so pages must meet Core Web Vitals.
- Programmatic SEO produces templated, **grounded** pages for use-case × industry × persona combinations via the [Content Factory](02-website/CONTENT_FACTORY.md).
- Each page must add real value (no thin or duplicate content) and pass the [Quality Gates](04-quality/QUALITY_GATES.md) plus a grounding check; rankings, impressions, CTR, and organic conversions are tracked in [Analytics](05-observability/ANALYTICS.md).
