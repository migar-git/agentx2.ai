## 2. Summary

- The content production swarm that fuels [SEO](02-website/SEO_STRATEGY.md) and thought leadership — grounded, evaluated, and human-gated before publish.
- Content agents: Blog (articles + posts), SEO (keywords + on-page), Research (grounded source material), Social (platform posts), Newsletter (digests), and Repurposing (one source → many formats).
- Pipeline: topic from clusters → grounded research → local-model draft → multi-eval + grounding check → human gate → publish (schema + sitemap) → analytics + learning.
- Quality rules: helpful, original, and grounded — no thin or fabricated content.
- Every piece passes the [Quality Gates](04-quality/QUALITY_GATES.md) (accessibility, performance, links, grounding) and is timestamped and cited.
- Originality and grounding are enforced under [Responsible AI](06-governance/RESPONSIBLE_AI.md).
- Performance feeds back via [Analytics](05-observability/ANALYTICS.md) and the [Learning Log](08-knowledge/LEARNING_LOG.md).
