For the broader visual and brand language, see the companion [Visual System](VISUAL_SYSTEM.md).

## 2. Summary

- The visual language: design tokens, components, and theme.
- Aesthetic target: executive, AI-native, finance-grade, enterprise, premium, and future-facing — dark-mode-first and modern-minimal.
- Design tokens are the single source of truth (color, type, spacing, radius, motion) and are consumed via the Tailwind theme config.
- Theme: dark mode first with light supported; both meet WCAG 2.2 AA contrast.
- Component library: buttons/links (44px min targets, clear focus rings), cards, global nav + footer, the AI widget shell, forms, and tables/pricing.
- Principles: accessible by default, responsive (mobile-first), fast (no heavy frameworks on the critical path), and consistent (tokens only, no ad-hoc values).
- The [Visual System](VISUAL_SYSTEM.md) extends this front door with the wider brand and visual identity.
